    Dried apricots for weight loss-minus 1 kg for 1 day

    Dried apricots are dried apricots that contain a large number of useful microelements and vitamins for the body.

    Dried apricots are used for weight loss and to restore the capacity of the muscles after strong physical exertion. Dried apricot – an inexhaustible source of potassium, an element that is extremely necessary for heart diseases, as well as useful for people during the rehabilitation period for their full recovery.

    Despite the fact that 100 g of dried fruit contains more than 200 calories, nutritionists have developed a special diet based on this product.

    Diet with kuraga for weight loss

    Many people doubt the possibility of using dried apricots in dietary nutrition in order to get rid of excess weight. After all, often for giving a taste to dried apricots before sale additionally sweeten (sometimes-honey, but most often-molasses), and there is an excess of fructose in it. Nevertheless, such a diet exists, and what is especially valuable, it has proven its effectiveness.

    The essence of this diet is that it helps to overcome the constant need of the body for sweets, which it experiences after a low-carb restrictive diet.

    You can use a diet based on dried apricots, after overeating, when the next day there is heaviness and discomfort in the stomach, and throughout the entire gastrointestinal tract. In this case, such a useful property of this dried fruit as stimulation works intestinal peristalsis and the ability to purify the stomach.

    However, there may also be a side effect of eating dried apricots – a strong gas formation caused by the abundance of fructose in it. Fasting day with dried apricots it is best to assign for the weekend, since it has a strong laxative effect.

    Dried apricots are accepted for weight loss as a diet only for one day, since they are deprived of protein. Therefore, in order to avoid the occurrence of disorders in the metabolic system in the body, the diet with kuraga can not last more than one day.

    The diet of this brief the diet consists of 200 g of dried apricots. All berries should be divided into 5 portions. Each portion should be eaten with water or tea, if you feel hungry. In addition, you should drink 2-3 liters of water per day, which is necessary to stimulate the cleansing processes in the kidneys.

    According to another recipe, you should take 300 g of dried apricots and 200 ml of freshly squeezed juice-puree (or pure juice) of apricots, chop all this mixture in a blender, divide the resulting product into five portions and eat it every three hours.

    Use of dried apricots in the diet

    Some nutritionists advise everyone who wants to lose weight, baking, sweets or just sugar replace it with kuraga. But simply removing sweet foods from the diet will not give the desired result.

    It is possible to lose weight only when the overall diet, lifestyle and amount of dried apricots consumption is changed.

    There must be a measure in everything, and this diet involves certain restrictions:

    • you can eat no more than 20-30 g of dried apricots per day;
    • dried apricots can be eaten only up to 14 hours, to give the body the opportunity to "burn" carbohydrate fuel due to physical exertion and prevent its reserves in the liver, so that then they do not turn into fat scurf;
    • drink unsweetened tea and coffee;
    • dried apricots should be eaten only at one meal as a dessert, preferably at lunch, and not for Breakfast, so as not to increase your appetite before dinner;
    • do not combine dried apricots with white bread, rice, pasta, sweets, or yogurt in one meal;
    • nutrition it should be balanced, and the daily diet should include two protein, low-fat dishes, two servings of cereals, five servings of vegetable dishes or fresh vegetables and fruits.
