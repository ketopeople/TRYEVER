Life Plan-Live for today while planning for tomorrow!

A Life Plan Community, formerly known as a Continuing Care Retirement Community (CCRC), ensures that you’re prepared if your health needs change.

Independent living at Monarch Landing offers an active and engaging lifestyle, free from cares and responsibilities of home ownership. Here, you’ll meet like-minded people with whom you can explore hobbies and interests, great dining, and other fabulous amenities that you so richly deserve.

If your health needs change, The Springs at Monarch Landing makes the transition to more comprehensive care available under the same roof.

The Springs provides:

Short-term solutions:

  • Rehabilitation
  • Respite care
  • Respite rehabilitation program

Long-term solutions:

  • Memory support assisted living
  • Enriched living
  • Long-term care

As a Life Plan Community, we provide the security of knowing that your future is provided for, even if your needs change.
